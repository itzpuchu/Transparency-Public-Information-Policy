Rules are airtight with no loopholes. Mistakes happen, but progress is always visible.

Staff Rules & Expectations

Staff guidelines are public to maintain accountability and transparency. Players may submit feedback if staff members violate rules.

  • Prioritize responsibilities over gameplay
  • Maintain professionalism
  • Follow hierarchy rules
  • Never leak confidential info
  • Report unhandled bugs
  • Helpers: Warn, Kick, Mute
  • JrMods: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access
  • Mods: Guide Helpers & JrMods
  • SrMods: Mentor lower staff, advanced permissions
  • Admins+: Full permissions, confidential commands

NovaQuest In-Game Rules

All players must follow rules to ensure fair, safe, and fun gameplay. Ignorance is not an excuse.

Unfair Advantage / Competitive

  • No hacked clients, macros, or exploits.
  • AFK automation, boosting, X-Ray, Fly, KillAura, etc. prohibited.
  • Regular players: progressive bans (3d → 7d → 14d)
  • Competitive: 3-day ban + stat wipe + leaderboard removal + season ban

IRL Trading & Gambling

  • No IRL trading outside NovaQuest
  • No player-run casinos or gambling
  • NovaQuest-approved events are allowed

NovaQuest Discord Rules

All members must follow rules to maintain a safe, respectful, and fun community.

  • Respect all members
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • No spam or advertising
  • Follow staff instructions
  • No harassment or discrimination in voice channels
  • Keep discussions respectful and appropriate
