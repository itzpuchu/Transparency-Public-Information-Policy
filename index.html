NovaQuest Staff Rules & Expectations

Staff are held to a higher standard than players. Players can submit feedback if staff violate these rules.

General Duties

  • Prioritize staff responsibilities over gameplay.
  • Maintain professional interactions with all members.
  • Report bugs within 24 hours. Exploiting unreported bugs is prohibited.
  • Do not leak any confidential information (staff tickets, Discord, internal discussions).
  • All promotions are decided by management; hints or promises are forbidden.

Hierarchy & Responsibilities

  • Helpers: Answer questions, report bugs. Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute.
  • JrMods: Handle exploiters and moderate issues. Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Tools.
  • Mods: Guide Helpers & JrMods. Issue and reverse standard punishments.
  • SrMods: Mentor lower staff. Reviewed for Admin promotion.
  • Admins+: Full permissions, confidential commands. Leaks → immediate removal.

NovaQuest In-Game Rules

  • Ignorance of the rules is not a valid excuse.
  • No using cheat software or unfair advantage: fly, X-ray, macros, nukers, AFK automation, boosting others.
  • Alternative accounts are forbidden without founder approval. Multiple players on the same IP must submit a ticket.
  • Server attacks, DDoS, IP grabbing, or malicious links → IP ban + report to Mojang & host.
  • IRL trading is prohibited; attempting or suggesting counts as a network removal offense.
  • Gambling or player-run casinos are forbidden unless NovaQuest-approved.

Unfair Advantage & Competitive

  • Regular players: bans escalate depending on offense, no stat wipes unless severe.
  • Competitive: 3-day ban + stat removal + leaderboard removal + competitive ban for the season.
  • Punishments escalate: 1st offense: 3d, 2nd: 7d, 3rd: 14d.

NovaQuest Discord Rules

  • Be respectful; no harassment, bullying, or discrimination.
  • No NSFW content in non-NSFW channels.
  • No spamming, advertising, or impersonation.
  • Voice chat must remain respectful and safe for all.
  • Bot commands must not be abused.
  • Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans.

Transparency Policy

As the founder of NovaQuest, all staff actions, rules, and decisions are public to ensure trust and fairness. Punishments, appeals, and rule changes are visible so players see accountability and progress. This approach ensures the community knows we prioritize safety, fairness, and enjoyment.
