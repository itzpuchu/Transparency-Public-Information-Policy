As the founder of NovaQuest, all relevant information will be made public to ensure fairness and accountability. This approach guarantees that the community can trust both staff and leadership decisions.

Transparency ensures:

  • Players know what to expect from staff
  • Punishments are consistent
  • Appeals and progress are visible

Rules and policies are designed with no loopholes, while allowing staff and players to learn from mistakes and maintain a fair, safe, and fun environment.

NovaQuest Staff Rules & Expectations

This staff guide is publicly available to ensure transparency and accountability. Players can provide feedback if a staff member is believed to violate these guidelines.

  • Prioritize staff responsibilities over gameplay.
  • Maintain professionalism in all interactions.
  • Follow hierarchy rules.
  • Do not hint at promotions.
  • Report bugs within 24 hours; exploiting unreported bugs is a strikeable offense.
  • Never leak confidential staff information.
  • Helpers: Warn, Kick, Mute
  • JrMods: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access
  • Mods: Guide lower staff, reverse standard punishments if necessary
  • SrMods: Mentor lower staff, advanced permissions
  • Admins+: Full permissions, confidential commands

NovaQuest In-Game Rules

Rules are designed to keep gameplay fair, safe, and enjoyable for everyone. Ignorance is not an excuse.

Unfair Advantage / Competitive Violations

  • Use of hacked clients or macros is strictly prohibited.
  • AFK automation, boosting, X-Ray, Fly, KillAura, etc. are banned.
  • Regular players: progressive bans (1st → 3d, 2nd → 7d, 3rd → 14d)
  • Competitive: 3-day ban + stat wipe + leaderboard removal + season ban

IRL Trading & Gambling

  • Trading or selling items/services outside NovaQuest is forbidden.
  • Player-run casinos or gambling are prohibited.
  • NovaQuest-run events are allowed if compliant with EULA.

NovaQuest Discord Rules

All members must follow rules to maintain a safe, respectful, and fun environment.

  • Be respectful: no harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.
  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination in voice channels.
  • Keep conversations respectful and appropriate for all members.
