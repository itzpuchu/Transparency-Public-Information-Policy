Transparency & Public Information Policy

As the founder of NovaQuest, I am committed to making all information public to the community. Before NovaQuest, I worked as staff on multiple servers where founders were not transparent. This will not happen here.

By sharing information openly, both staff and players can build trust and work together effectively. All decisions, including in-game rules, staff expectations, punishments, and appeals, will be public. This ensures:

  • Players know exactly what to expect from staff
  • Punishments are clear and consistent
  • Appeals show the second chances we provide

Our goal is to maintain fairness, transparency, and clarity, preventing loopholes while keeping the server safe and enjoyable for everyone.

NovaQuest Staff Rules & Expectations

This guide is publicly available to outline staff expectations and allow players to give feedback if they believe a staff member violates these guidelines.

  • Prioritize staff responsibilities over gameplay.
  • Maintain professionalism at all times.
  • Do not hint at promotions; management and founder decide all promotions.
  • Report bugs within 24 hours; using unreported bugs is a strikeable offense.
  • Do not leak confidential information; leaking → instant demotion/removal.
  • Do not close tickets prematurely unless player is uncooperative or hasn’t replied in 24 hours.
  • Helpers: Answer questions, report bugs. Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute.
  • JrMods: Handle exploiters, ban-worthy issues. Permissions: Warn, Kick, Mute, Ban, System Access.
  • Mods: Guide Helpers & JrMods, ensure full understanding of rules and mechanics.
  • SrMods: Mentor lower staff, accountable for training, reviewed for Admin promotion.
  • Admins+: Handle all issues, oversee compliance. Leaks → demotion/removal.
  • 3 strikes per staff, reset every 6 months, logged permanently.
  • Accumulation/severity may lead to demotion or removal.
  • Strikeable offenses: impolite behavior, misuse of commands, failure to report bugs, leaking info.
  • Management monitors engagement; no strict minimum.
  • Inactivity >3 months may result in demotion; possible reinstatement at last role.
  • SrMods must maintain high activity; demotion possible without guaranteed reinstatement.

NovaQuest In-Game Rules

  • Cheat software, hacked clients, automation (X-Ray, Fly, KillAura, Macros, etc.)
  • AFK grinding or failing staff checks intentionally
  • Boosting other players or leaderboard manipulation
  • Alternative accounts require founder approval; multiple people on same IP must submit a ticket
  • Server attacks (lagging, DDoS, malicious links) → IP ban + report to Mojang & host

Punishment: 3-Day Ban + Stat Wipe + Leaderboard Removal + Competitive Ban

  • First offense: 3 days
  • Second offense: 7 days
  • Third offense: 14 days
  • Trading with real money or external services is strictly prohibited.
  • Attempting, suggesting, or offering counts as a violation.
  • Player-run casinos/gambling are prohibited per EULA.
  • Mentioning casinos or gambling is not allowed unless official NovaQuest-approved.

NovaQuest Discord Rules

  • Be respectful to everyone.
  • No harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No spamming or excessive caps.
  • No advertising other servers.
  • No harassment or discrimination in voice chat.
  • Keep conversations respectful and appropriate.
  • No spamming bot commands or abusing bot systems.
  • Follow instructions for bot usage.
